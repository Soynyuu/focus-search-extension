# Privacy Policy

Focus Search does not collect, store, sell, transmit, or analyze personal data.

The extension stores one local setting: whether Focus Search is on or off. This setting is saved with `chrome.storage.local` and never leaves the user's browser.

Focus Search does not run analytics, does not make external network requests, does not use cookies, and does not operate outside `youtube.com`.

Search terms entered in the Focus Search screen are sent directly to youtube.com's normal search results URL. They are not sent to the extension author or any third-party service.

Focus Search is independent and is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or sponsored by YouTube or Google.
